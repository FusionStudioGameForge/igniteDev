PIP’s unlucky day

Pip goes with his parents to go camping, 
his father says he needs to go get more firewood for the campfire, his mom goes with and he is told to not go wandering off.
Pip finds a suspicious looking rock and goes to investigate it,
he falls into a cave and needs to find his way out by navigating through different caves to make it back to the top.
Help Pip get back to the surface.
